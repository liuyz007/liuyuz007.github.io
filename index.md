I am currently a Master's student majoring in Control Engineering at [Anhui University](https://www.ahu.edu.cn/), Hefei, China. My research focuses on solving challenging problems in visual perception and computational imaging. Specifically, my research expertise lies in **computer vision**, **low-level vision**, **low-light image enhancement**, and **deep learning**. 

I earned my B.E. degree in Automation from Anhui University in June 2024. Building upon a solid foundation in automation and control systems, I am passionate about developing efficient algorithms and applying AI to real-world engineering challenges. I am highly proficient in programming languages such as **Python** and **C**, and I have extensive hands-on experience with mainstream deep learning frameworks and tools, including **PyTorch**, **TensorFlow**, **OpenCV**, and **Hugging Face**.

I am currently actively seeking **internship opportunities** in related fields. If you are interested in my background, please feel free to contact me via email.
